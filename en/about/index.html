About mwum

“mwum” originates from the Chinese “明无梦,” which is a non-standard pinyin abbreviation. The meaning in Chinese is:

In the future, I had a dream named “Nothing”.
In the past, I also had such dreams.
I don’t know if I am dreaming now.
Maybe everything is essentially nothing.
The world is inherently nothing, it’s us who regard nothing as something, isn’t it all the same?
